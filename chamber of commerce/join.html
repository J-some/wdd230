Why Join Us?

The 7 benefits to join the Chamber of Commerce:

  1. Networking opportunities
  2. Gain a voice in the Municipality
  3. Make business contacts
  4. Receive discounts for being a membership
  5. Get customer referrals
  6. Bring new customers to your business
  7. Increase your influence in the community

Membership Benefits by Level

$100 - Non-profit Level:

Ideal for new businesses and small business.

$200 - Bronze Level:

Ideal for businesses with a few employees and medium business.

$400 - Silver Level

Recommended for businesses with more than 2 branches. Provides some business trainings and social media posts.

$1,000 - Gold Level

Recommended for businesses with more than 3 branches, this membership provides full business trainings, social media posts, luncheon tickets, sponsorships with levels of advertising, etc.
